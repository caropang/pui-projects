About Us

Our bakery started out in Pittsburgh in 2008.

Ever since, we have continued our misson to serve our famous cinnamon rolls to our customers. For the first time, we are offering delivery of our fresh cinnamon rolls!

We use the highest quality, locally sourced ingredients, and have stuck to our original family recipie which has been perfected over generations. Over the years, we have grown our range of flavor offerings, and have special seasonal flavors at differnt times of the year.
